Mission 1 - Secure Your Star System

  • Click on Map - the map will show you your home planet
  • Pinch zoom out to see your home system and a few other planets.
  • Single click on planet to open planet detail window
  • Click on "Make Factory" to order a factory.
  • In single player mode, the game will automatically finish the turn for your and let the enemy do their turn.
  • Zoom into your home planet and click on the factory
  • Make 5 pods and 1 cruiser. Click on each ship in turn and set the pods destination to the other planets in your system. You need to select a destination in the dropdown.
  • Mission Success: Population on all planets. Armed ships (i.e. cruiser) around all planets.

Mission 2 - Explore Other Systems. Exterminate the enemy.

  • Zoom out all the way to see more star systems. If you turn on Hyper-Sensors in options, you will soon see other ships from other races. They are not friendly.
  • Make cruisers and send them to the closest other system as defense. The cruisers will shoot down pods and enemy cruisers once they are in reach.
  • Follow up with a few pods and start populating another star system. Once you have population on a planet, you can build a factory there and build cruisers locally.
  • To win this mission, you need to destroy the enemy head quarters. Send a few cruisers into the orbit of the enemy home world and they will take care of business.
