Andromeda Rising is a turn-based strategy game. As the leader of your race you manage your planet, build ships, eXplore your star system, eXpand to nearby systems, eXploit any resources you find, and eXterminate anyone in your way (4X).

This game is in early release and has limited game play. Please do not install if you do not want to be an early tester.

Main Screen

  • Play - Play the current game. Disabled until you load a mission or a game.
  • Save Game - Save your current game. Disabled until implemented.
  • Load Game - Load a new Game. Choose between single player missions, two player Pass and Play maps, or a demo mode.
  • Stats - Your achievements.
  • Help - Loads this text.
  • Options - Change settings.
  • Do - Skip or finish your turn and let the enemies do their turn.

Two Player Pass and Play Mode

The game allows two players to play against each other by passing the device back and forth.

To speed up the game, players need not pass after every action. Players can play a few turns ahead (currently ~10). If e.g. player 1 plays 5 turns ahead, they will automatically skip the next 5 turns. That means after player 2 played their first turn action, the game will automatically finish the first turn, and player 2 gets to play again. This goes on until player 2 finished the fifth turn. At that point, they can pass the device back, or play ahead on their own.

FAQ

  • What about feature X?
    Check out the road map.
  • Why don't my ships shoot at enemy ships passing through a star system?
    It may look like they fly through the system, but they are way above or below in the galactic planet. One way to tell if a Pod is in-system is to watch it's trail. If it's straight, it may be heading elsewhere. If it curves toward a planet then it's in-system. Another way to tell is by looking closely at the color. Deep space ships are more muted than in-system.
  • What are the circles around some ships?
    An experimental feature shows click targets. If it has a circle, you can click it. You can toggle this in options (Show Click Targets)
  • What's up with the graphics?
    All graphics assets are currently place holders. The ship and race pictures have been drawn by a local aspiring artist. If you are a graphics designer and have cycles to spare, feel free to reach out to me.
  • What is the background
    The background is Hubble's picture of the Orion Nebula. Hubble took pictures of Andromeda, too. But Orion is more dramatic.
  • Why is there a GB on some screens?
    It's a reference to Galactic Bloodshed, an 80/90 online multi-user 4X space themed game.
