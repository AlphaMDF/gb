Options

  • Hyper Sensors - show you all ships anywhere in the universe. Turned off, you can only see enemy ships if they are in the same system as you.
  • Show Perf Stats - Displays various performance related stats.
  • Show Race Stats - Displays stats about each races in the Universe.
  • Show Click Targets - Displays the circle that registers clicks.
  • Show Continuous Do Button - Display Fast Forward button that lets you automatically skip your turns until you press it again. While pressed, go to the map and zoom all the way out to see other races' ships exploring. Beware of the beetles.
