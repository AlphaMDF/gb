Navigating the Map
  • Double click on stars, planets and ships panzooms to the target. Double click on empty space zooms out.
  • Single click on stars, planets, and ships brings up the details window. Single click on empty space closes it.
  • Drag, pinch-zoom, and double-click-drag pans, zooms, and panzooms the map.
  • (+) in a detail window pan-zooms to what is described in the window. Click on (*) to pan-zoom to its star.
  • Double click on a planet pins the planet in a "geo-centric" view. Everything will now move around the planet. Double click on a star, or zoom out to go back to regular heliocentric mode.
